# 01. What business GlobiGuard is in

## Core answer

GlobiGuard is in the **AI authority layer** business.

That means it governs:

1. whether sensitive data may cross a model boundary
2. whether an action may run
3. whether a workflow may continue automatically
4. whether a human must review
5. whether evidence exists to prove what happened

## The right framing

The product is not primarily:

- a filter
- a set of libraries
- a dashboard
- a compliance report generator

Those are delivery surfaces.

The product is the **decision authority between AI intent and real-world execution**.

## What customers are really buying

Customers are buying:

- **permissioning for AI actions**
- **trust-boundary enforcement**
- **auditability**
- **operational control**
- **deployment choice**

They are paying to reduce four risks:

1. data leakage
2. unauthorized action execution
3. workflow drift
4. unprovable compliance posture

## What this means in business terms

GlobiGuard is closest to:

- security infrastructure
- policy infrastructure
- runtime governance infrastructure
- enterprise control plane software

It is **not** best understood as an application feature library.

## Economic consequence

If GlobiGuard is treated as a library business, it gets priced like a commodity.

If GlobiGuard is treated as an authority/control-plane business, it gets priced like critical infrastructure:

- higher willingness to pay
- stronger expansion potential
- deeper integration
- more operational dependency
- longer customer life
