# GlobiGuard business pack

## One-line answer

**GlobiGuard is in the AI authority infrastructure business.**

Not the library business.  
Not the compliance PDF business.  
Not the generic workflow-builder business.

The durable business is: **becoming the control plane that decides whether AI-driven actions may happen, under what policy, with what evidence, and inside whose trust boundary.**

## Why this matters

If you think you are selling:

- SDKs
- React components
- n8n nodes
- compliance exports

then you will optimize for distribution but not for power.

If you think you are selling:

- governed execution
- policy control
- human review
- auditability
- tenant trust boundaries
- enterprise deployment choice

then you are building a category with higher switching costs, better expansion paths, and more long-term value.
