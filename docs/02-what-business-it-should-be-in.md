# 02. What business it should be in

## Recommendation

GlobiGuard should be in the **private trust and control plane for AI operations** business.

That is the deeper business.

## Why this is the right business

The long-term winners in infrastructure are rarely the people who only expose the edge interface. They win by owning the **system of record and system of control** behind the interface.

For GlobiGuard, that system is:

- policies
- runtime decisions
- approval state
- evidence
- deployment identity
- model and connector governance

## The category to build

The category is not "AI safety tool."

That is too generic and too easy to collapse into features.

The category is closer to:

**AI authority infrastructure**

or

**governed AI execution infrastructure**

or

**trust control plane for AI workflows**

## The strategic position

GlobiGuard should sit where:

1. applications and agents want to act
2. enterprises need control
3. workflows need continuity
4. auditors need proof

That makes it the layer customers do not want to rip out once it is wired in.

## What to monetize

The durable paid center should be:

1. **control plane**
   - policy lifecycle
   - environment management
   - approvals
   - audit state
   - package and runtime identity

2. **managed trust operations**
   - private tenant hosting
   - upgrade management
   - key issuance and rotation
   - support and SLAs

3. **enterprise authority features**
   - advanced policy packs
   - compliance mappings
   - deployment assurance
   - premium connectors and workflow governance

## What should be open

Open-source should help distribution and trust:

- SDKs
- adapters
- bootstrap tooling
- reference integrations
- documentation

## What should stay scarce

Scarcity should live in:

- managed control plane
- network of enterprise trust
- policy intelligence
- audit and approval workflow product
- enterprise deployment and support motion

## Best long-term shape

The best shape is:

- open integration layer
- proprietary or monetized authority layer
- enterprise-grade deployment choices

That gives:

- low-friction adoption
- strong expansion revenue
- real switching costs
- better longevity than a pure API wrapper company
